## 3. Transfer of Ownership and Delivery

3.1 **Transfer of title.** On payment of the Purchase Price (or, if the Parties

## 4. Seller's Representations and Warranties

4.1 **Ownership.** The Seller represents that the Seller is the lawful owner of the
Property and has the full right and authority to sell it.

4.2 **Free of liens.** The Seller represents that the Property is free and clear of
all liens, security interests, and encumbrances, except: **[LIST ANY LIENS TO BE
PAID OFF AT OR BEFORE SALE, or state "None."]**.

4.3 **Authority.** The Seller represents that signing and performing this Bill of
Sale does not violate any agreement or obligation binding on the Seller.

4.4 **Accuracy.** The Seller represents that the description of the Property in
Section 1 is accurate to the best of the Seller's knowledge.

## 5. Condition of the Property

5.1 **As-is sale.** Except for the representations in Section 4 and any warranty
required by applicable law, the Property is sold **"AS IS" and "WITH ALL FAULTS,"**
and the Seller disclaims all other warranties, express or implied, including any
implied warranty of merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose, to the
fullest extent permitted by law.

5.2 **Buyer's inspection.** The Buyer acknowledges that the Buyer has had the
opportunity to inspect the Property (or has knowingly waived inspection) and is
relying on the Buyer's own examination, except for the Seller's representations in
Section 4.

## 6. Indemnification

6.1 **By Seller.** The Seller will indemnify the Buyer against any third-party
claim arising from the Seller's ownership of the Property before the transfer or
from a breach of the Seller's representations in Section 4.

6.2 **By Buyer.** The Buyer will indemnify the Seller against any claim arising
from the Buyer's ownership or use of the Property after the transfer.

## 7. General Provisions

7.1 **Entire agreement.** This Bill of Sale is the entire agreement between the
Parties on its subject and supersedes prior discussions. It may be amended only by
a writing signed by both Parties.

7.2 **Governing law and venue.** This Bill of Sale is governed by the laws of the
State of **[STATE]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws rules. The Parties
submit to the jurisdiction of the courts located in **[COUNTY, STATE]**.

7.3 **Severability and waiver.** If any provision is unenforceable, the rest
remains in effect. A Party's failure to enforce a provision is not a waiver.

7.4 **Assignment.** This Bill of Sale binds and benefits the Parties and their
heirs, successors, and permitted assigns.

7.5 **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Bill of Sale may be signed in
counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and all of
which together form one document.
