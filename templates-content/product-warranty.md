## 2. What Is Covered

2.1 **Warranty.** Company warrants that, under normal use and service, the Product
will be free from defects in materials and workmanship for the warranty period
stated in Section 3.

2.2 **Conformity.** Company warrants that the Product will conform in all material
respects to its published specifications and accompanying documentation at the
time of delivery.

2.3 **Standard of performance.** "Normal use" means use consistent with the
Product's documentation and intended purpose, in ordinary operating conditions,
and excludes the uses described in Section 5.

3.1 **Duration.** The warranty period is **[NUMBER, e.g. 12]** months from the
date of original purchase (the "**Warranty Period**"), unless a longer period is
required by applicable law or stated on the Product or its packaging.

3.2 **Replacement parts.** Any repaired or replacement part or Product is
warranted for the remainder of the original Warranty Period or
**[NUMBER, e.g. 90]** days from the date of repair or replacement, whichever is
longer.

3.3 **No extension.** Performing warranty service does not extend the original
Warranty Period except as stated in Section 3.2.

## 4. Remedy

4.1 **Company's options.** If the Product is defective during the Warranty Period
and Customer makes a valid claim, Company will, at its option and expense,
(a) repair the Product, (b) replace it with a comparable new or refurbished
product, or (c) refund the purchase price.

4.2 **Sole remedy.** The remedies in Section 4.1 are Customer's exclusive remedy
for breach of this Warranty, except for any non-waivable rights under applicable
law.

4.3 **Return of defective Product.** Customer may be required to return the
defective Product, or proof of its defect, before Company provides a remedy.
Company will provide return instructions through the claim process in Section 6.

4.4 **Shipping.** **[Company / Customer]** pays shipping for warranty returns
within the Warranty Period, as described in the claim instructions.

## 5. What Is Not Covered

5.1 **Exclusions.** This Warranty does not cover problems caused by:
(a) accident, abuse, misuse, neglect, or improper handling; (b) use inconsistent
with the Product's documentation; (c) unauthorized repair, modification, or
opening of the Product; (d) normal wear and tear, including consumable parts;
(e) damage from power surges, fluids, fire, or acts of nature; or (f) cosmetic
damage that does not affect function.

5.2 **Excluded items.** This Warranty does not cover accessories, batteries, or
software except as expressly stated, and does not cover any product not
manufactured or authorized by Company.

5.3 **Effect of misuse.** Damage falling within Section 5.1 voids coverage for the
affected components, but does not void this Warranty for unrelated defects.
