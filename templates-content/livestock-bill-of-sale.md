## 4. Delivery, Possession, and Transfer of Title

4.1 **Conveyance.** Effective on Seller's receipt of the full Purchase Price (or
cleared funds), Seller sells, transfers, and conveys to Buyer all of Seller's
right, title, and interest in the Livestock.

4.2 **Delivery or pickup.** Delivery will occur by **[BUYER PICKUP / SELLER
TRANSPORT / CARRIER]** at **[LOCATION]** on or before **[DELIVERY DATE]**. The
Party responsible for transport and its cost is: **[ALLOCATION]**.

4.3 **Risk of loss.** Risk of loss, injury, illness, or death of the Livestock
passes to Buyer upon **[DELIVERY / PICKUP]**, except to the extent caused by
Seller's negligence before that time.

4.4 **Care during transition.** Until risk passes, Seller will provide ordinary,
humane care, feed, and water for the Livestock.

## 5. Seller's Representations and "As-Is" Sale

5.1 **Ownership and authority.** Seller represents that Seller is the lawful owner

5.2 **Disclosures.** Seller has disclosed any known vices, behavioral issues,
injuries, lameness, or prior medical conditions of which Seller is aware:
**[DISCLOSURES / NONE KNOWN]**.

5.3 **As-is sale.** Unless Section 5.4 states otherwise, the Livestock is sold
**"AS IS,"** with all faults, and Seller makes no warranty of any kind, express or
implied, including as to soundness, breeding ability, performance, or fitness for a
particular purpose, except for the representations in this Section.

5.4 **Limited warranty or guarantee (optional).** If the Parties agree to a
specific guarantee (for example, a breeding soundness, live-foal, or short-term

5.5 **Inspection.** Buyer acknowledges that Buyer has had the opportunity to
inspect the Livestock and to obtain a pre-purchase veterinary examination by a
veterinarian of Buyer's choosing, and is relying on Buyer's own inspection and
judgment except as to the matters represented in this Section.

## 6. Indemnification and Allocation of Risk

6.1 **By Seller.** Seller will indemnify and hold Buyer harmless from claims,
liabilities, and reasonable costs arising from Seller's ownership or care of the
Livestock, or any lien or title defect, attributable to the period **before** risk
of loss passes.

6.2 **By Buyer.** Buyer will indemnify and hold Seller harmless from claims,
liabilities, and reasonable costs arising from Buyer's ownership, possession, care,
transport, or use of the Livestock attributable to the period **on or after** risk
of loss passes, including injury caused by the Livestock to persons or property.

6.3 **Cooperation.** Each Party will promptly notify the other of any claim covered
by this Section and provide reasonable cooperation in its defense.

## 7. General Provisions

7.1 **Governing law.** This Bill of Sale is governed by the laws of the State of
**[STATE]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws rules. Animal health, brand
inspection, transport, and tax requirements vary by state and, for interstate
movement, by federal rules; the Parties will comply with the requirements of the
applicable jurisdictions.

7.2 **Entire agreement.** This Bill of Sale, together with any schedule of animals
attached to it, is the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the sale of
the Livestock and supersedes any prior discussions. It may be amended only by a
writing signed by both Parties.

7.3 **Severability.** If any provision is held unenforceable, the remaining
provisions remain in full force and effect.

7.4 **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Bill of Sale before the transfer
of ownership is complete without the other Party's prior written consent.

7.5 **Notices.** Notices under this Bill of Sale must be in writing and sent to the
addresses stated above (or as updated in writing) and are effective on receipt.

7.6 **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Bill of Sale may be signed in
counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and all of
which together form one document.
