This Room Rental Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is entered into as of
**[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "**Effective Date**") by and between:

**[OWNER / PRIMARY TENANT LEGAL NAME]**, who owns or holds the lease to the
residence described below, with a notice address at **[ADDRESS]** ("**Housing
Provider**"); and

**[ROOM RENTER LEGAL NAME]**, with a notice address at **[ADDRESS]**
("**Renter**").

Housing Provider and Renter are each a "**Party**" and together the "**Parties**."

**Recitals.** Housing Provider owns or lawfully occupies the residence described
below and wishes to rent a private room within it, together with shared use of
common areas, to Renter; and Renter wishes to rent that room, on the terms below.
In consideration of the mutual promises below, the Parties agree as follows.

## 1. Room and Shared Areas

1.1 **Rented room.** Housing Provider rents to Renter the private room located at
**[DESCRIBE ROOM, e.g. "the bedroom on the second floor, northwest corner"]** in
the residence at **[FULL ADDRESS]** (the "**Room**").

1.2 **Shared areas.** Renter has the non-exclusive right to use the following
shared areas in common with the Housing Provider and any other residents:
**[e.g. kitchen, living room, bathrooms, laundry, yard, parking]** (the
"**Shared Areas**").

1.3 **Exclusive vs. shared.** The Room is for Renter's exclusive use. The Shared
Areas are used in common, and Housing Provider may set reasonable rules for their
use under Section 4.

1.4 **Furnishings.** The Room is **[furnished as listed in [EXHIBIT] /
unfurnished]**. Renter is responsible for the condition of furnishings provided,
ordinary wear and tear excepted.

## 2. Term

2.1 **Term.** This Agreement begins on **[START DATE]** and **[continues
month-to-month until terminated under Section 7 / ends on [END DATE]]**.

2.2 **Renewal.** **[OPTIONAL: The term renews automatically on a month-to-month
basis after [END DATE] unless either Party gives notice under Section 7.]**

## 3. Rent, Deposit, and Costs

3.1 **Rent.** Renter will pay Housing Provider rent of **[AMOUNT]** per month (the
"**Rent**"), due in advance on the **[first]** day of each month, without demand.

3.2 **What rent includes.** Rent **[includes / does not include]** the following
utilities and services: **[e.g. electricity, water, gas, internet, trash]**. Any
shared costs not included in Rent will be split **[equally among residents / as
follows: DESCRIBE]**.

3.3 **Security deposit.** On signing, Renter will pay a security deposit of
**[AMOUNT]** (the "**Security Deposit**"). Housing Provider may apply it to unpaid
Rent and damage beyond ordinary wear and tear, and will return the balance, with
any itemization and within the time required by applicable law, after Renter
vacates. Deposit rules vary by jurisdiction; confirm local requirements.

3.4 **Late charges.** If Rent is not received within **[NUMBER]** days after its
due date, Renter will pay a late charge of **[AMOUNT OR %]**, to the extent
permitted by applicable law.

## 4. House Rules and Conduct

4.1 **Quiet hours and conduct.** Renter will respect quiet hours of **[TIMES]**,
keep noise reasonable, and treat other residents with respect. Renter will not
create a nuisance or use the residence for any unlawful purpose.

4.2 **Guests.** Overnight guests are permitted **[as follows: e.g. up to [NUMBER]
nights per month with advance notice]**. Renter is responsible for the conduct of
Renter's guests.

4.3 **Cleanliness.** Renter will keep the Room and Renter's portion of the Shared
Areas clean and will share reasonable responsibility for common cleaning as
**[described here / agreed among residents]**.

4.4 **Smoking, pets, and substances.** **[Smoking is prohibited / permitted only
in DESCRIBE.] [Pets are not permitted without written consent / permitted as
follows.]** Unlawful drug activity is prohibited.

4.5 **Use of Shared Areas.** Renter will not store personal items in Shared Areas
beyond what Housing Provider permits and will not alter the residence without
Housing Provider's prior written consent.

## 5. Access and Privacy

5.1 **Renter privacy.** Housing Provider will respect Renter's reasonable privacy
in the Room and will not enter the Room except with Renter's consent, after the
notice required by applicable law, or in an emergency.

5.2 **Locks and keys.** Housing Provider will provide Renter keys or access
devices to the residence and Room. Renter will not change locks without Housing
Provider's prior written consent and will return all keys and devices at the end
of the term.

## 6. Maintenance and Damage

6.1 **Habitability.** Housing Provider will maintain the residence in the
condition required by applicable habitability and housing law and will make
repairs that are Housing Provider's responsibility within a reasonable time after
notice.

6.2 **Renter responsibility.** Renter will promptly report needed repairs and is
responsible for damage to the Room, Shared Areas, or residence caused by Renter or
Renter's guests beyond ordinary wear and tear.

## 7. Termination

7.1 **Notice to terminate.** For a month-to-month term, either Party may terminate
by giving written notice at least the number of days required by applicable law
(commonly **[NUMBER, e.g. 30]** days). For a fixed term, this Agreement ends on
the end date unless renewed. Notice requirements are governed by the law of the
residence's location.

7.2 **Move-out.** On termination, Renter will remove belongings, return all keys
and devices, and leave the Room and Renter's portion of the Shared Areas clean and
in the condition required by this Agreement, ordinary wear and tear excepted.

7.3 **Default.** If Renter fails to pay Rent when due or materially breaches this
Agreement and does not cure within any period required by applicable law after
written notice, Housing Provider may terminate and pursue remedies available under
applicable law. Self-help eviction is prohibited; Housing Provider will use lawful
process where required. Whether a room-renter arrangement is a tenancy or a
lodger arrangement, and the process to end it, vary by jurisdiction; confirm local
rules.

## 8. General Provisions

8.1 **Governing law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of
**[STATE]** and the local ordinances applicable to the residence.

8.2 **Notices.** Notices must be in writing and delivered to the addresses above
(or as updated in writing) by a method permitted under applicable law.

8.3 **Entire agreement; amendment.** This Agreement is the entire agreement
between the Parties on its subject and may be amended only by a writing signed by
both Parties.

8.4 **Severability and waiver.** If any provision is unenforceable, the rest
remains in effect. A Party's failure to enforce a provision is not a waiver.

8.5 **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Agreement may be signed in
counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and all of
which together form one agreement.
