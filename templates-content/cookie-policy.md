## 1. What Cookies Are

1.1 **Definition.** A "**Cookie**" is a small text file placed on your device when
you visit a website or use an application. Cookies allow the Service to recognize
your device, store information about your session and preferences, and operate
reliably.

1.2 **Similar technologies.** This Policy also covers technologies that perform
functions similar to cookies, including pixels, web beacons, local storage,
software development kits, and device identifiers (together with Cookies,
"**Tracking Technologies**").

1.3 **First-party and third-party.** "**First-party**" Tracking Technologies are
set by the Company. "**Third-party**" Tracking Technologies are set by other
parties, such as analytics or advertising providers, whose own privacy and cookie
notices govern their use of the information they collect.

## 2. Categories of Cookies We Use

2.1 **Strictly necessary.** These Tracking Technologies are required for the
Service to function, including authentication, security, load balancing, and
remembering items you have selected. The Service cannot operate properly without
them, and they generally cannot be switched off in our systems.

2.2 **Functional.** These Tracking Technologies remember choices you make, such as
language, region, and display preferences, to provide enhanced and personalized
features. Disabling them may degrade certain functionality.

2.3 **Performance and analytics.** These Tracking Technologies collect information
about how Users interact with the Service, such as which pages are visited and
whether errors occur, so we can measure and improve performance. The information
is **[aggregated / pseudonymized]** where feasible.

2.4 **Advertising and targeting.** **[IF APPLICABLE: These Tracking Technologies
are used to deliver advertising relevant to you and to measure the effectiveness
of campaigns. They may be set by us or by advertising partners and may be used to
build a profile of your interests.]**

## 3. Why We Use Cookies

3.1 **Purposes.** We use Tracking Technologies to: (a) operate and secure the
Service; (b) authenticate Users and prevent fraud; (c) remember preferences; (d)
analyze usage and improve features; and (e) where applicable and permitted,
deliver and measure advertising.

3.2 **No undisclosed purposes.** We do not use Tracking Technologies for purposes
materially different from those described in this Policy without updating this
Policy and, where required, obtaining your consent.

## 4. Duration of Cookies

4.1 **Session cookies.** "**Session Cookies**" are temporary and are deleted when
you close your browser or app. We use them to support core functions during a
single visit.

4.2 **Persistent cookies.** "**Persistent Cookies**" remain on your device until
they expire or you delete them. We use them to remember preferences and recognize
returning Users. Retention periods vary by Cookie and are designed to be no longer
than necessary for the stated purpose.
