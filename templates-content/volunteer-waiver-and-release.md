This Volunteer Waiver and Release (this "**Release**") is entered into as of
**[DATE]** (the "**Effective Date**") by and between:

**[ORGANIZATION LEGAL NAME]**, a **[STATE] [ENTITY TYPE, e.g. nonprofit
corporation]** with its principal office at **[ORGANIZATION ADDRESS]** (the
"**Organization**"); and

**[VOLUNTEER FULL LEGAL NAME]**, an individual residing at **[VOLUNTEER
ADDRESS]** (the "**Volunteer**").

The Organization and the Volunteer are each a "**Party**" and together the
"**Parties**."

**Recitals.** The Volunteer wishes to participate, without compensation, in
volunteer activities organized by or on behalf of the Organization (the
"**Activities**"), which may include **[DESCRIBE ACTIVITIES, e.g. event setup,
trail maintenance, food distribution, construction work, transportation]**. The
Volunteer understands that the Activities may involve risks of injury, illness,
property damage, or other loss. In consideration of being permitted to
participate, the Volunteer agrees to the following, and the Parties agree as
follows. The enforceability of liability waivers varies by jurisdiction, and
some claims (including for gross negligence or willful misconduct) generally
cannot be waived.

---

## 1. Voluntary Participation

1.1 **No compensation.** The Volunteer participates in the Activities voluntarily
and without expectation of wages, salary, or other compensation, and is not an
employee of the Organization.

1.2 **No employment relationship.** Nothing in this Release creates an employment,
agency, partnership, or joint-venture relationship. The Volunteer is not entitled
to employee benefits, workers' compensation (except as required by law), or
unemployment benefits arising from the Activities.

1.3 **Right to decline.** The Volunteer may decline to participate in any
Activity and may stop participating at any time, and the Organization may decline
or end the Volunteer's participation at any time for any lawful reason.

## 2. Assumption of Risk

2.1 **Acknowledgment of risks.** The Volunteer understands that the Activities may
involve inherent and other risks, including **[DESCRIBE FORESEEABLE RISKS, e.g.
slips, trips, and falls; lifting injuries; exposure to weather, tools, equipment,
or hazardous materials; transportation risks; and contact with the public]**.

2.2 **Voluntary assumption.** The Volunteer knowingly and voluntarily assumes all
such risks, both known and unknown, and accepts personal responsibility for any
injury, illness, death, property damage, or other loss arising from
participation, except to the extent caused by the Organization's gross negligence
or willful misconduct or as otherwise provided by law.

2.3 **Physical condition.** The Volunteer represents that the Volunteer is in
sufficient health and physical condition to participate safely and will inform the
Organization of any condition that may affect the Volunteer's safe participation.

## 3. Release and Waiver of Liability

3.1 **Release.** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, the Volunteer
releases, waives, and discharges the Organization and its directors, officers,
employees, agents, and other volunteers (the "**Released Parties**") from any and
all claims, demands, causes of action, and liabilities for any injury, illness,
death, property damage, or other loss arising out of or related to the
Volunteer's participation in the Activities.

3.2 **Limits on the release.** This release does not apply to claims that may not
lawfully be released, including, in many jurisdictions, claims arising from the
Released Parties' gross negligence, recklessness, or willful or wanton
misconduct. The validity and scope of this release are governed by applicable
law and may be construed more narrowly than stated.

3.3 **Covenant not to sue.** The Volunteer agrees not to bring any claim against
the Released Parties for any matter released above, and acknowledges that this
Release is intended to be binding on the Volunteer's heirs, executors,
administrators, and assigns.

## 4. Indemnification

4.1 **Indemnity by Volunteer.** To the extent permitted by law, the Volunteer
will indemnify and hold harmless the Released Parties from any third-party claim
arising out of the Volunteer's negligent or wrongful acts or omissions during the
Activities, or the Volunteer's breach of this Release.

4.2 **Cooperation.** The Parties will cooperate in the defense of any claim
subject to indemnification and will not settle a claim that imposes liability on
the other Party without that Party's consent.

## 5. Medical Treatment and Emergencies

5.1 **Consent to treatment.** In the event of injury or illness during the
Activities, the Volunteer consents to receive first aid and, if the Volunteer
cannot consent at the time, authorizes the Organization to arrange emergency
medical treatment. The Volunteer is responsible for the cost of any medical
treatment.

5.2 **Emergency contact.** The Volunteer's emergency contact is **[NAME,
RELATIONSHIP, PHONE]**.

5.3 **No medical insurance provided.** The Organization does not provide medical
or health insurance for the Volunteer, and the Volunteer is encouraged to
maintain the Volunteer's own coverage.

## 6. Media Release

6.1 **Use of likeness.** The Volunteer **[GRANTS / DOES NOT GRANT]** the
Organization permission to photograph, film, or record the Volunteer during the
Activities and to use the Volunteer's name, image, voice, and likeness in the
Organization's promotional, educational, and fundraising materials, without
compensation.

6.2 **Revocation.** The Volunteer may revoke this media permission prospectively
by written notice, which does not affect materials already created or
distributed.

## 7. Rules, Conduct, and Confidentiality

7.1 **Rules.** The Volunteer agrees to follow the Organization's safety rules,
codes of conduct, and the reasonable directions of Organization staff during the
Activities.

7.2 **Conduct.** The Volunteer will act safely, respectfully, and lawfully, and
will not engage in any conduct that endangers others or harms the Organization's
reputation.

7.3 **Confidentiality.** The Volunteer will keep confidential any non-public
information about the Organization, its beneficiaries, donors, or operations that
the Volunteer learns through the Activities, both during and after the
Volunteer's service.

## 8. General Provisions

8.1 **Governing law and venue.** This Release is governed by the laws of the State
of **[STATE]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws rules, and any dispute
will be brought exclusively in the courts located in **[COUNTY, STATE]**.

8.2 **Severability.** If any provision of this Release is held unenforceable, the
remaining provisions remain in effect, and the unenforceable provision will be
enforced to the greatest extent permitted by law.

8.3 **Entire agreement; amendment.** This Release is the entire agreement between
the Parties on its subject and may be amended only by a writing signed by both
Parties.

8.4 **Voluntary and informed.** The Volunteer acknowledges that the Volunteer has
read this Release, understands it, has had the opportunity to ask questions and
seek independent advice, and signs it freely.

8.5 **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Release may be signed in
counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and all of
which together form one agreement.
