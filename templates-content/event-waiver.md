# EVENT WAIVER AND RELEASE OF LIABILITY

> Replace every **[BRACKETED]** field with your specifics, delete or adapt any
> clause that does not fit your situation, and have a licensed attorney in the
> governing jurisdiction review it before you or anyone else signs. CyberSygn

>
> This document type carries significant legal and/or financial consequences and
> varies substantially by jurisdiction. Having a licensed attorney review it
> before use is strongly recommended.

This Event Waiver and Release of Liability (this "**Waiver**") is entered into as
of **[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "**Effective Date**") by and between:

**[PARTICIPANT NAME]**, residing at **[PARTICIPANT ADDRESS]** (the
"**Participant**"); and

**[EVENT ORGANIZER / HOST NAME]**, a **[STATE] [ENTITY TYPE / individual]** with
its principal place of business or residence at **[ADDRESS]** (the
"**Organizer**").

Participant and Organizer are each a "**Party**" and together the "**Parties**."

**Recitals.** The Organizer is hosting **[EVENT NAME / DESCRIPTION: e.g. a 5K run,
a festival, a tournament, a conference, a community fair]** (the "**Event**") at
**[VENUE / LOCATION]** on or about **[EVENT DATE(S)]**. The Participant wishes to
attend or take part in the Event and understands that attendance and participation
involve certain risks. The Organizer permits the Participant to attend and take
part only on the condition that the Participant agrees to this Waiver. In
consideration of being permitted to attend and participate, the Parties agree as
follows.

---

## 1. The Event and Voluntary Participation

1.1 **Description.** The Event consists of **[DESCRIPTION OF THE EVENT AND THE
PARTICIPANT'S ROLE: e.g. spectator, registered participant, competitor,
volunteer, vendor]** and takes place at **[VENUE]** during **[DATE(S) AND
TIMES]**.

1.2 **Voluntary participation.** The Participant attends and takes part in the
Event voluntarily and of the Participant's own free will and may decline to
participate in any portion of the Event.

1.3 **Eligibility.** The Participant meets any eligibility requirements stated by
the Organizer for the Event, including **[e.g. minimum age, registration, waiver
on file, health requirements]**.

## 2. Assumption of Risk

2.1 **Inherent and other risks.** The Participant understands that attending and
participating in the Event involves risks, including **[e.g. crowds, slips, trips
and falls, physical exertion, contact with other participants, weather exposure,
vehicle and equipment movement, food and beverage service, and acts of third
parties]**, and that these risks may result in property damage, minor or serious
bodily injury, illness, permanent disability, or death.

2.2 **Knowing assumption.** Knowing and appreciating these risks, the Participant
freely and voluntarily assumes all risk of loss, damage, injury, illness, or
death arising out of or related to attending or participating in the Event,
including risks arising from the Organizer's ordinary negligence to the extent
permitted by law.

2.3 **Personal property.** The Participant is responsible for the Participant's own
personal property at the Event. The Organizer is not responsible for lost,
stolen, or damaged personal property.

## 3. Release and Waiver of Claims

3.1 **Release.** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, the
Participant releases, waives, and discharges the Organizer and its owners,
officers, employees, agents, sponsors, venue operators, volunteers, and
contractors (the "**Released Parties**") from any and all claims, demands, causes
of action, and liability for loss, damage, injury, illness, or death arising out
of or related to attending or participating in the Event, including claims based on
the ordinary negligence of any Released Party.

3.2 **Covenant not to sue.** The Participant agrees not to sue or bring any claim
against the Released Parties for any matter released under Section 3.1.

3.3 **Limits of the release.** This release does not apply to liability that
applicable law does not permit to be waived, including, in many jurisdictions,
liability for gross negligence, recklessness, or willful or wrongful misconduct.
The scope and enforceability of liability waivers vary by jurisdiction; local law
controls where it limits what may be released.

## 4. Indemnification

4.1 **Indemnity.** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, the
Participant will indemnify and hold harmless the Released Parties from any claim
brought by or on behalf of the Participant, or by a third party, arising out of the
Participant's attendance or participation, or the Participant's breach of this
Waiver, including reasonable attorneys' fees.

4.2 **Damage caused by Participant.** The Participant is responsible for any
damage the Participant causes to the venue, equipment, or property of the Released
Parties or other attendees.

## 5. Event Rules and Conduct

5.1 **Compliance.** The Participant agrees to follow all Event rules, posted
signage, venue policies, and reasonable instructions of the Organizer and its
staff and security.

5.2 **Prohibited conduct.** The Participant will not engage in conduct that
endangers others, will not bring prohibited items onto the premises, and will not
participate while impaired by alcohol or drugs in a way that creates risk.

5.3 **Removal for cause.** The Organizer may refuse entry to, or remove, the
Participant for violating Event rules or for conduct that endangers others, without
refund of any fee.

## 6. Medical Authorization

6.1 **Consent to treatment.** In the event of injury or illness at the Event, the
Participant authorizes the Organizer to arrange emergency medical care and
transport. The Participant is responsible for the cost of any medical treatment and
related transport.

6.2 **Disclosure.** The Participant has disclosed any medical condition, allergy,
or limitation relevant to safe attendance and participation: **[LIST OR
"NONE"]**.

## 7. Photography and Media

7.1 **Consent to media.** The Participant **[CONSENTS / DOES NOT CONSENT]** to
being photographed, filmed, or recorded at the Event and to the Organizer's use of
those images for promotional and operational purposes without further
compensation. Delete or adapt this Section if no media consent is sought.

## 8. Refunds and Cancellation

8.1 **Refund policy.** Registration fees are **[REFUNDABLE / NON-REFUNDABLE /
REFUNDABLE ONLY AS STATED]**. The Organizer's stated refund and transfer policy
applies.

8.2 **Event changes.** The Organizer may reschedule, relocate, shorten, or cancel
the Event due to weather, safety, force majeure, or low participation, and will
handle fees in accordance with Section 8.1.

## 9. Minor (if applicable)

9.1 **Parent or guardian consent.** If the Participant is under the age of
majority in the governing jurisdiction, the undersigned parent or legal guardian
represents that they have legal authority to consent on the Participant's behalf,
agrees to all terms of this Waiver on the Participant's behalf, and, to the fullest
extent permitted by law, releases and indemnifies the Released Parties on the same
terms as the Participant.

9.2 **Acknowledgment.** The parent or guardian understands that, in some
jurisdictions, a parent's pre-injury waiver of a minor's claims may be limited or
unenforceable, and that local law controls.

## 10. General Provisions

10.1 **Governing law and venue.** This Waiver is governed by the laws of the State
of **[STATE]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws rules. The Parties submit to
the jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in **[COUNTY, STATE]**.

10.2 **Severability.** If any provision of this Waiver is held unenforceable, the
remaining provisions remain in full force, and the unenforceable provision will be
narrowed or reformed only to the extent needed to make it enforceable, so that the
release operates to the maximum extent the law allows.

10.3 **Entire agreement; amendment.** This Waiver is the entire agreement between
the Parties on its subject and may be amended only by a writing signed by both
Parties.

10.4 **Binding effect.** This Waiver binds and benefits the Parties and their
heirs, next of kin, executors, administrators, successors, and assigns.

10.5 **Acknowledgment of understanding.** The Participant has read this Waiver,
understands that it gives up substantial legal rights, including the right to sue,
and signs it freely and voluntarily.

10.6 **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Waiver may be signed in
counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and all of
which together form one agreement.
