These Short-Term Rental House Rules (these "**House Rules**") are part of, and are
incorporated by reference into, the booking confirmation and rental terms for the
property located at **[PROPERTY ADDRESS]** (the "**Property**"), and are agreed to
as of **[BOOKING DATE]** by and between:

**[HOST LEGAL NAME]**, the owner or authorized manager of the Property
("**Host**"); and

**[GUEST LEGAL NAME]**, the individual who booked the stay and who is responsible
for all occupants and visitors ("**Guest**").

Host and Guest are each a "**Party**" and together the "**Parties**."

**Recitals.** Host has agreed to allow Guest to occupy the Property on a
short-term basis for the dates of the confirmed reservation (the "**Stay**").
Guest wishes to occupy the Property and agrees to follow these House Rules, which
exist to protect the Property, neighbors, and future guests, and to comply with
applicable local short-term rental, noise, occupancy, and safety requirements. In
consideration of the confirmed reservation and the mutual promises below, the
Parties agree as follows.

---

## 1. Reservation and Occupancy

1.1 **Authorized dates.** Guest may occupy the Property only during the confirmed
check-in and check-out dates and times: check-in is **[CHECK-IN TIME]** on
**[CHECK-IN DATE]** and check-out is **[CHECK-OUT TIME]** on **[CHECK-OUT DATE]**.
Early arrival or late departure is permitted only with Host's prior written
approval and may incur an additional charge.

1.2 **Maximum occupancy.** No more than **[NUMBER]** persons, including children
and infants, may stay overnight at the Property. Occupancy in excess of this limit
is a material breach of these House Rules and grounds for immediate termination of
the Stay without refund.

1.3 **Registered guest responsibility.** Guest is the responsible adult for the
Stay, must be at least **[18 / 21]** years of age, and is liable for the conduct
of every occupant and visitor. Guest will ensure that all occupants and visitors
read and follow these House Rules.

1.4 **Visitors.** Day visitors are **[PERMITTED UP TO [NUMBER] PERSONS / NOT
PERMITTED]**. Overnight visitors beyond the registered occupants are not permitted
without Host's prior written consent. Visitors are not permitted to use
**[POOL / HOT TUB / GYM / OTHER]** without Host approval.

## 2. Conduct, Noise, and Neighbors

2.1 **Quiet hours.** Guest will observe quiet hours from **[QUIET HOURS START,
e.g. 10:00 PM]** to **[QUIET HOURS END, e.g. 8:00 AM]**. During quiet hours, noise
must not be audible at the property line.

2.2 **No parties or events.** Parties, events, and large gatherings are
prohibited. The Property may not be used for any commercial filming, photo shoot,
or promotional event without Host's prior written consent.

2.3 **Respect for neighbors.** Guest will be considerate of neighbors at all
times, will not trespass on adjacent property, and will comply with any homeowners
association or building rules provided by Host.

2.4 **Local ordinances.** Guest will comply with all applicable local noise,
nuisance, parking, occupancy, and short-term rental ordinances. Guest is
responsible for any fine or penalty assessed against the Property as a result of
Guest's, an occupant's, or a visitor's conduct.

## 3. Smoking, Substances, and Pets

3.1 **Smoking.** Smoking, vaping, and use of any tobacco or cannabis product
inside the Property is strictly prohibited. **[Smoking is permitted only in the
designated outdoor area at [LOCATION] / Smoking is prohibited anywhere on the
Property.]** A cleaning and deodorizing charge of **[AMOUNT]** applies to any
violation.

3.2 **Controlled substances.** No illegal drugs or illegal activity of any kind is
permitted at the Property.

3.3 **Pets.** Pets are **[PERMITTED, SUBJECT TO THE PET ADDENDUM AND A PET FEE OF
[AMOUNT] / NOT PERMITTED]**. Service animals required by law are accommodated as
required by applicable law. Undisclosed pets are a breach of these House Rules and
may result in additional cleaning charges.

## 4. Care of the Property

4.1 **General care.** Guest will treat the Property and its furnishings with care
and will keep the Property reasonably clean during the Stay. Guest will not move
furniture between rooms or remove any item from the Property.

4.2 **Damage and loss.** Guest is responsible for any damage to the Property,
furnishings, appliances, or grounds caused by Guest, an occupant, or a visitor
beyond normal wear and tear, and authorizes Host to charge the cost of repair or
replacement against the security deposit or payment method on file.

4.3 **Reporting.** Guest will promptly notify Host of any damage, malfunction,
spill, or maintenance issue so it can be addressed for Guest's comfort and for
future guests.

4.4 **Utilities and conservation.** Guest will use heating, cooling, water, and
electricity reasonably. Windows and doors should be closed while climate control
is running. Excessive utility use inconsistent with normal residential occupancy
may incur an additional charge as disclosed at booking.

## 5. Safety and Security

5.1 **Locking up.** Guest will lock all doors and windows and engage any alarm
when leaving the Property and overnight.

5.2 **Keys, codes, and access.** Guest will not duplicate keys, share access
codes, or grant access to anyone other than registered occupants. Lost keys or
unreturned access devices incur a re-keying charge of **[AMOUNT]**.

5.3 **Safety equipment.** Guest will not disable, cover, or remove smoke alarms,
carbon-monoxide detectors, fire extinguishers, or any other safety device. Guest
will report any non-functioning safety device to Host immediately.

5.4 **Pools, hot tubs, and hazards.** Use of any **[POOL / HOT TUB / WATERFRONT /
FIRE PIT]** is at Guest's own risk. Children must be supervised at all times.
Guest will follow all posted safety rules. Guest assumes the risks inherent in
these amenities to the fullest extent permitted by applicable law.

## 6. Check-Out and Charges

6.1 **Check-out procedure.** Before departure, Guest will: (a) start the
dishwasher or wash used dishes; (b) place trash and recycling in the designated
bins; (c) return furniture and keys to their original location; and (d) follow any
additional written check-out instructions Host provides.

6.2 **Cleaning fee.** A cleaning fee of **[AMOUNT]** is charged as disclosed at
booking. Excessive mess, biohazard cleanup, or trash removal beyond ordinary
turnover cleaning may incur an additional charge.

6.3 **Security deposit.** A refundable security deposit or hold of **[AMOUNT]** may
apply. Host will return the deposit, less any lawful deductions itemized in
writing, within the time required by applicable local law.

6.4 **Lost and found.** Items left behind will be held for **[NUMBER]** days. Host
will return items at Guest's request and expense; unclaimed items may be donated
or discarded after that period.

## 7. Violations, Liability, and General Terms

7.1 **Enforcement.** Violation of these House Rules is a material breach. Host may,
consistent with applicable law, require the violating party to leave, terminate
the Stay without refund, and pursue any charges or damages permitted by the rental
terms and applicable law.

7.2 **Assumption of risk and release.** To the fullest extent permitted by
applicable law, Guest occupies and uses the Property and its amenities at Guest's
own risk and releases Host from liability for injury, loss, or damage that is not
caused by Host's gross negligence or willful misconduct. This provision does not
waive any right that cannot be waived under applicable law.

7.3 **Indemnification.** Guest will indemnify and hold Host harmless from claims,
fines, and expenses arising from the conduct of Guest, an occupant, or a visitor,
or from any breach of these House Rules, except to the extent caused by Host's
gross negligence or willful misconduct.

7.4 **Governing law.** These House Rules are governed by the laws of the State of
**[STATE]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws rules, and are subject to all
applicable short-term rental and consumer-protection rules of **[COUNTY, STATE]**.

7.5 **Entire understanding; severability.** These House Rules, together with the
booking confirmation and rental terms, are the entire understanding on their
subject. If any provision is unenforceable, the rest remains in effect.

7.6 **Acknowledgment.** By signing below or by checking in, Guest acknowledges
that Guest has read, understood, and agreed to these House Rules on behalf of all
occupants and visitors.
