## 1. Scope and Definitions

1.1 **Scope.** This Policy governs personal information we process as a controller
of that information in connection with the Service. It does not apply to
third-party websites, products, or services that we do not control, even if they
link to or from the Service.

1.2 **Personal Information.** "**Personal Information**" means information that
identifies, relates to, describes, or could reasonably be linked to an
identified or identifiable individual. It does not include de-identified,
aggregated, or publicly available information that cannot reasonably be
associated with an individual.

1.3 **Processing.** "**Processing**" means any operation performed on Personal
Information, including collection, use, storage, disclosure, and deletion.

1.4 **Sensitive Information.** "**Sensitive Information**" means categories of
Personal Information that applicable law treats as requiring heightened
protection, such as government identifiers, financial account credentials,
precise geolocation, health information, and information revealing protected
characteristics. We process Sensitive Information only where permitted by law and,
where required, with your consent.

2.1 **Information you provide.** We collect Personal Information you submit

2.3 **Cookies and tracking technologies.** We and our service providers use
cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to operate the Service, remember your

can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or any consent
mechanism we make available.

2.4 **Information from third parties.** We may receive Personal Information from
third parties such as identity verification providers, analytics vendors,
advertising partners, and publicly available sources, which we may combine with
information we already hold.

3.4 **No use inconsistent with this Policy.** We will not use Personal Information
