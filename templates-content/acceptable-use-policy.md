## 1. Scope and Application

1.1 **Who is covered.** This Policy applies to all Users and to anyone who accesses
the Service through a User's account, including the User's personnel, agents, and
end users. You are responsible for ensuring that everyone who uses the Service
through you complies with this Policy.

1.2 **All content and activity.** This Policy applies to all content, data, code,
and communications that you submit to, store on, transmit through, or generate
using the Service ("**User Content**"), and to all conduct in connection with the
Service.

1.3 **Relationship to the Terms.** A violation of this Policy is a violation of the
Terms. Where this Policy conflicts with the Terms, the Terms control unless the
Terms state otherwise.

## 2. General Standards of Conduct

2.1 **Lawful use.** You will use the Service only for lawful purposes and in
compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and third-party rights.

2.2 **Authorized access.** You will access the Service only through the interfaces
and credentials we provide and only within the scope of access we authorize.

2.3 **Account security.** You will keep your credentials confidential, will not
share accounts except as we permit, and will promptly notify us of any suspected
unauthorized access or security incident.

2.4 **Accurate information.** You will provide accurate registration and contact
information and keep it current.

## 3. Prohibited Activities

3.1 **Illegal and harmful conduct.** You will not use the Service to engage in or
promote any activity that is illegal, fraudulent, deceptive, defamatory, harassing,
threatening, or that infringes or misappropriates the rights of others, including
intellectual property and privacy rights.

3.2 **Harmful content.** You will not upload, store, or transmit User Content that
is unlawful, obscene, sexually exploitative of minors, hateful, or that incites
violence, or that you do not have the right to share.

3.3 **Malicious code and interference.** You will not introduce malware, viruses,
or other harmful code; interfere with or disrupt the integrity or performance of
the Service; or attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service, its systems, or
related networks or accounts.

3.4 **Security testing.** You will not probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of
the Service or breach any security or authentication measures, except under a
written authorization or bug-bounty program we provide.

3.5 **Abuse of resources.** You will not use the Service in a way that imposes an
unreasonable or disproportionately large load on our infrastructure, circumvents
usage limits, or otherwise degrades the Service for others.

3.6 **Spam and unsolicited messages.** You will not use the Service to send spam,
unsolicited bulk or commercial messages, or messages that violate anti-spam or
electronic-communications laws.

3.7 **Misrepresentation.** You will not impersonate any person or entity,
misrepresent your affiliation, or forge headers or identifiers to disguise the
origin of any content transmitted through the Service.

## 4. Data and Privacy Responsibilities

4.1 **Lawful data handling.** You will collect, use, and share any personal
information processed through the Service in compliance with applicable
data-protection laws and your own privacy notices, and you will obtain any required
consents.

4.2 **No prohibited data.** Unless we expressly agree in writing, you will not
submit to the Service any sensitive categories of data that require special
handling under applicable law, such as government identifiers, payment card data
outside an approved integration, or protected health information.

4.3 **Third-party rights.** You will not use the Service to process data in a way
that violates the rights of the individuals to whom the data relates or the terms
under which you obtained the data.

## 5. Acceptable Use of Communications and Networks

5.1 **Communications tools.** If the Service includes messaging, email, or other
communications features, you will use them only to send content that is lawful,
solicited or permitted, and consistent with this Policy.

5.2 **No interception or harvesting.** You will not intercept, monitor, or collect
information about other Users without authorization, and you will not harvest or
scrape data from the Service except as expressly permitted.

5.3 **API and automation.** If you access the Service through an API or automated
means, you will comply with any applicable rate limits, documentation, and
authentication requirements, and will not use automation to circumvent restrictions.

## 6. Enforcement

6.1 **Monitoring.** We may, but are not obligated to, monitor use of the Service and
User Content to enforce this Policy and protect the Service and its Users, subject
to applicable law and our privacy notices.

6.2 **Investigation.** We may investigate suspected violations and cooperate with
law enforcement and other authorities where appropriate.

6.3 **Remedies.** If we determine that you have violated this Policy, we may, with
or without notice as the circumstances warrant: (a) remove or disable access to
offending User Content; (b) suspend or limit your access to the Service; (c)
terminate your account or the Terms; and (d) pursue any other remedy available at
law or under the Terms.

6.4 **Emergencies.** We may take immediate action, including suspension, where we
reasonably believe it is necessary to prevent harm to the Service, to us, or to
others, and will provide notice as soon as practicable where feasible.
