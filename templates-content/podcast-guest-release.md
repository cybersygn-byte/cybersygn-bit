This Podcast Guest Release (this "**Release**") is entered into as of
**[EFFECTIVE DATE]** (the "**Effective Date**") by and between:

**[PRODUCER LEGAL NAME]**, an individual or **[STATE] [ENTITY TYPE]** that
produces the podcast known as **[PODCAST NAME]** (the "**Producer**"); and

**[GUEST LEGAL NAME]**, an individual with a mailing address at
**[GUEST ADDRESS]** (the "**Guest**").

Producer and Guest are each a "**Party**" and together the "**Parties**."

**Recitals.** Guest has agreed to appear as a guest on one or more episodes of
the podcast known as **[PODCAST NAME]** (the "**Podcast**"), and the Parties wish
to confirm Producer's right to record, edit, publish, and distribute the
recording. In consideration of Guest's appearance, the publicity to Guest, and
the mutual promises below, the Parties agree as follows.

---

## 1. The Recording

1.1 **Appearance.** Guest will participate in a recorded interview or
conversation for the Podcast on or about **[RECORDING DATE]** regarding the topic
**[TOPIC]** (the "**Recording**").

1.2 **Recording defined.** The "**Recording**" includes the audio and, if
applicable, video capture of Guest's participation, together with Guest's name,
voice, likeness, image, statements, biographical information, and any materials
Guest provides for use in the episode (the "**Guest Materials**").

1.3 **Format.** The Recording may be released as **[audio only / audio and video
/ clips]** and may be edited, excerpted, and combined with other content at
Producer's discretion, consistent with this Release.

## 2. Grant of Rights

2.1 **License and consent.** Guest grants Producer a perpetual, irrevocable,
worldwide, royalty-free, sublicensable, and transferable right and license to
record, edit, reproduce, publish, distribute, publicly perform, display,
promote, and otherwise exploit the Recording and Guest Materials in any media now
known or later developed.

2.2 **Name and likeness.** Guest consents to Producer's use of Guest's name,
voice, likeness, image, and biographical information in connection with the
Recording, the Podcast, and the promotion of each, including in episode titles,
descriptions, show notes, social media, and advertising.

2.3 **Edits.** Guest acknowledges that Producer may edit the Recording for
length, clarity, and quality and is not obligated to publish the Recording or
any portion of it.

2.4 **No approval right.** Except as the Parties expressly agree in **Exhibit A**
(for example, an agreed review of designated clips), Guest has no right to
approve the final edit, and Producer retains editorial control.

## 3. Ownership

3.1 **Producer ownership.** Producer owns all right, title, and interest in the
Recording and the resulting episode, including the copyright in the produced
work, except for Guest's pre-existing rights in Guest Materials, which Guest
licenses under Section 2.

3.2 **Guest's pre-existing materials.** Guest retains ownership of any
pre-existing works Guest provides (such as slides, music, or images) and grants
Producer a license to use them as part of the Recording and its promotion.

3.3 **No partnership.** This Release does not make the Parties partners or
employees of one another and does not entitle Guest to royalties or revenue from
the Podcast unless Exhibit A states otherwise.

## 4. Compensation

4.1 **Consideration.** Unless Exhibit A states a fee, Guest appears without
monetary compensation, and the consideration to Guest is the opportunity to
participate and the associated exposure.

4.2 **Optional fee or perks.** If Exhibit A provides a fee, honorarium, travel
reimbursement, or other benefit, Producer will provide it as described there.

4.3 **No future obligation.** This Release does not obligate Producer to invite
Guest to future episodes or to provide ongoing promotion.

## 5. Guest Representations and Warranties

5.1 **Authority.** Guest represents that Guest is at least the age of majority in
Guest's jurisdiction (or that a parent or guardian has signed) and has the right
to grant the rights in this Release.

5.2 **Own statements.** Guest represents that Guest's statements during the
Recording are Guest's own, are made in good faith, and do not knowingly
infringe, defame, or violate the rights of any third party.

5.3 **Guest Materials.** Guest represents that Guest owns or has the right to
provide the Guest Materials and that Producer's use of them as permitted will not
infringe third-party rights.

5.4 **Confidential information.** Guest is responsible for not disclosing the
confidential information of any third party during the Recording.

## 6. Producer Obligations

6.1 **Lawful use.** Producer will use the Recording in a manner consistent with
this Release and applicable law and will not knowingly use the Recording to
defame Guest or to falsely attribute statements to Guest.

6.2 **Attribution.** Producer will identify Guest by the name and descriptors
Guest provides, subject to reasonable editorial discretion.

6.3 **Reasonable takedown.** If Guest notifies Producer in writing of a genuine
factual error or a good-faith privacy or safety concern, Producer will consider
the request in good faith, though Producer is not obligated to remove published
content except as required by law.

## 7. Release and Waiver

7.1 **Release.** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, Guest
releases Producer and its licensees and assigns from any claim arising out of the
authorized use of the Recording and Guest Materials, including claims for
defamation, invasion of privacy, right of publicity, or emotional distress,
based on uses permitted by this Release.

7.2 **No inspection requirement.** Guest waives any right to inspect or approve
the finished Recording or the contexts in which it appears, except as expressly
provided in Exhibit A.

7.3 **Moral rights.** To the extent permitted by applicable law, Guest waives
moral rights in Guest's contribution as embodied in the Recording; where such
rights cannot be waived, Guest agrees not to assert them against Producer or its
licensees.

## 8. Term and Termination

8.1 **Term.** This Release takes effect on the Effective Date and continues
perpetually with respect to the Recording, consistent with the grant in Section
2.

8.2 **Irrevocability.** Because Producer will invest in producing and publishing
the episode in reliance on this Release, the rights granted are irrevocable once
the Recording is created, except as required by applicable law.

8.3 **Pre-recording withdrawal.** Guest may withdraw consent in writing before
the Recording occurs; after the Recording, the grant of rights continues as
stated.

## 9. General Provisions

9.1 **Governing law and venue.** This Release is governed by the laws of the
State of **[STATE]**, without regard to conflict-of-laws rules, and the Parties
submit to the courts located in **[COUNTY, STATE]**.

9.2 **Entire agreement.** This Release, with Exhibit A, is the entire agreement
between the Parties on its subject and supersedes prior discussions about the
Recording.

9.3 **Amendment.** This Release may be amended only by a writing signed by both
Parties.

9.4 **Severability and waiver.** If a provision is unenforceable, the rest
remains in effect, and a Party's failure to enforce a provision is not a waiver.

9.5 **Assignment.** Producer may assign or sublicense this Release in connection
with distribution, syndication, or sale of the Podcast. Guest may not assign this
Release.

9.6 **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Release may be signed in
counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and all of
which together form one agreement.
