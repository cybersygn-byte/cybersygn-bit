The Provider and the Participant are each a "**Party**" and together the
"**Parties**."

**Recitals.** The Participant wishes to take part in **[DESCRIBE ACTIVITY — e.g.
fitness classes, a recreational sport, an obstacle course, a guided excursion,
equipment use]** (the "**Activity**") offered or operated by the Provider. The
Activity involves inherent risks. As a condition of being permitted to participate,
the Participant agrees to assume those risks and to release the Provider on the
terms below. In consideration of being allowed to participate, the Parties agree as
follows.

---

## 1. Definitions and Scope

1.1 **Released Parties.** "**Released Parties**" means the Provider and its owners,
officers, directors, employees, instructors, volunteers, agents, contractors, and
the owners and operators of any premises or equipment used for the Activity.

1.2 **Activity.** The Activity is described in the Recitals and includes all
related instruction, orientation, transportation, use of facilities and equipment,
and time spent on the premises before, during, and after the Activity.

1.3 **Scope of this Waiver.** This Waiver applies to the Activity on the date(s)
above and, unless the Provider issues a new waiver, to the Participant's continued
participation in the same type of Activity with the Provider.

## 2. Acknowledgment and Assumption of Risk

2.1 **Inherent risks.** The Participant understands that the Activity involves
inherent and other risks that can cause property damage, bodily injury, illness,
permanent disability, or death. These risks include, without limitation:
**[describe specific risks — e.g. falls, collisions, equipment failure, strenuous
exertion, weather, terrain, contact with others, and the negligence of other
participants]**.

2.2 **Voluntary participation.** The Participant chooses to participate voluntarily
with full knowledge of the risks and represents that the Participant is physically
fit and has no medical condition that would make participation unsafe, except as
disclosed to the Provider in writing.

2.3 **Assumption of risk.** The Participant knowingly and voluntarily assumes all
risks of the Activity, both known and unknown, even if arising from the ordinary
negligence of the Released Parties, to the fullest extent permitted by applicable
law.

2.4 **Rules and instructions.** The Participant agrees to follow all rules, safety
instructions, and directions of the Provider and to stop participating and notify
the Provider if conditions become unsafe or the Participant feels unwell.

## 3. Release and Waiver of Claims

3.1 **Release.** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, the Participant
releases and forever discharges the Released Parties from any and all claims,
demands, causes of action, damages, and liabilities arising out of or related to
the Participant's participation in the Activity, including those based on the
ordinary negligence of the Released Parties.

3.2 **Covenant not to sue.** The Participant agrees not to sue or bring any claim
against the Released Parties for any matter released under Section 3.1.

3.3 **Limits required by law.** This Waiver does not release any Released Party from
liability for gross negligence, recklessness, willful or wanton misconduct, or any
liability that applicable law does not permit to be waived. Where local law limits
or prohibits pre-injury releases — including releases by or on behalf of minors —
those limits control, and the remainder of this Waiver remains in effect.

3.4 **No reliance.** The Participant has not relied on any oral or written
representation about the safety of the Activity other than what is stated in this
Waiver.

## 4. Indemnification

4.1 **Indemnity.** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, the
Participant (and, for a minor, the Guardian) will indemnify and hold the Released
Parties harmless from any claim brought by or on behalf of the Participant, or by a
third party arising from the Participant's conduct during the Activity, including
reasonable attorneys' fees and costs.

4.2 **Exclusion.** This indemnity does not apply to claims arising from a Released
Party's gross negligence or willful misconduct, or as otherwise limited by
applicable law.

## 5. Medical Treatment and Emergency Authorization

5.1 **Authorization.** The Participant authorizes the Provider to arrange emergency
medical treatment if the Participant is injured and unable to consent, and agrees
to be responsible for the cost of that treatment.

5.2 **No medical assurance.** The Provider does not warrant the availability,
adequacy, or outcome of any first aid or emergency care.

5.3 **Disclosure.** The Participant has disclosed to the Provider any medical
condition, allergy, or medication relevant to participation, or confirms there are
none requiring disclosure.

6.2 **Opt-out.** The Participant may decline this media release by initialing here:
**[____]**, in which case Section 6.1 does not apply.

## 7. Minors (If Applicable)

7.1 **Guardian authority.** If the Participant is under the age of majority, the
Guardian represents that the Guardian has full legal authority to sign this Waiver
on the minor's behalf.

7.2 **Guardian assumption and release.** The Guardian, individually and on behalf
of the minor, assumes the risks in Section 2 and grants the release in Section 3 to
the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, recognizing that some jurisdictions
limit a guardian's ability to waive a minor's claims.

7.3 **Guardian indemnity.** The Guardian agrees to the indemnity in Section 4 with
respect to the minor's participation.

## 8. General Provisions

8.1 **Governing law and venue.** This Waiver is governed by the laws of the State
of **[STATE]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws rules. The Parties submit to
the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in
**[COUNTY, STATE]**.

8.2 **Severability.** If any provision of this Waiver is held unenforceable, that
provision will be enforced to the greatest extent permitted by law, and the
remaining provisions remain in full effect. The Parties intend the release to be as
broad as the law allows.

8.3 **Entire agreement; amendment.** This Waiver is the entire agreement between the
Parties on its subject and supersedes prior discussions. It may be amended only by a
writing signed by both Parties.

8.4 **No waiver.** A Party's failure to enforce a provision is not a waiver of that
or any other provision.

8.5 **Voluntary and informed.** The Participant (and Guardian, if applicable) has
read this Waiver, understands it is a release of legal rights and an assumption of
risk, has had the opportunity to ask questions and seek counsel, and signs it freely.

8.6 **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Waiver may be signed in
counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and all of
which together form one agreement.
