## 1. Definitions

1.1 **Images.** "**Images**" means all photographs, video, audio, film, digital
files, and other recordings of the Model created in connection with the Project,
together with any reproductions, derivatives, composites, retouched versions, and
edits of them.

1.2 **Likeness.** "**Likeness**" means the Model's name, image, voice,
appearance, physical attributes, and persona as captured in the Images.

1.3 **Media.** "**Media**" means all media and formats now known or later
developed, including print, digital, broadcast, streaming, social media, and
display.

## 2. Grant of Rights

2.1 **License and consent.** The Model irrevocably grants Producer, and its
licensees, successors, and assigns, the perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free right
and license to use, reproduce, edit, publish, display, distribute, and exploit the
Images and the Model's Likeness in all Media.

2.2 **Permitted uses.** The Images may be used for **[SELECT / DESCRIBE: e.g.
advertising, marketing, editorial, portfolio, social media, commercial products,
stock licensing]**. Unless limited in Section 2.3, Producer may use the Images for
any lawful purpose consistent with these permitted uses.

2.3 **Limitations on use.** The following limitations apply (delete if none):
**[e.g. no use in connection with unlawful, defamatory, pornographic, or
politically endorsing content; no resale as a standalone stock image; usage
limited to the campaign described]**. Subject to those limitations, the Model
waives any right to inspect or approve the Images or the finished use.

2.4 **Edits and alterations.** The Model agrees that the Images may be cropped,
retouched, color-corrected, composited, or otherwise altered, and consents to
such use, except for alterations that are defamatory or that the limitations above
prohibit.

2.5 **Name and credit.** Producer **[IS / IS NOT]** required to credit the Model.
If credit is required, it will read **[CREDIT LINE]** where reasonably practical.

## 3. Compensation

3.1 **Consideration.** In exchange for this Release, the Model acknowledges
receipt of **[$ AMOUNT / "the agreed session fee" / "good and valuable
consideration, the sufficiency of which is acknowledged"]**, which the Model
agrees is full and complete compensation for the rights granted.

3.2 **No further payment.** Except as stated above, the Model is not entitled to
any further payment, royalty, or residual for any use of the Images, regardless of
how widely or for how long they are used.

## 4. Model's Representations

4.1 **Authority.** The Model is of legal age in the governing jurisdiction and has
the full right and authority to enter into this Release and grant the rights in
it. (If the Model is a minor, the parent or guardian signature block below must be
completed.)

4.2 **No conflicts.** The granting of these rights does not conflict with any
other agreement binding on the Model, and the Model has not granted any exclusive
rights that would prevent this Release.

4.3 **Voluntary.** The Model has read this Release, understands it, and is signing
it voluntarily. The Model has had the opportunity to seek independent advice.

## 5. Waiver and Release of Claims

5.1 **Release.** To the fullest extent permitted by law, the Model releases and
discharges Producer and its licensees, successors, and assigns from any and all
claims arising out of the authorized use of the Images and Likeness, including
claims for invasion of privacy, rights of publicity, defamation, and any claim
based on blurring, distortion, alteration, or use in composite form.

5.2 **No reversion.** The Model acknowledges that the rights granted are
irrevocable and that the Model has no right to terminate this Release or to recall
Images already used or licensed, subject to the limitations in Section 2.3.

## 6. Minor (if applicable)

6.1 **Parent or guardian consent.** If the Model is under the age of majority in
the governing jurisdiction, the undersigned parent or legal guardian represents
that they have the legal authority to consent on the Model's behalf, agrees to all
terms of this Release on the Model's behalf, and personally makes the
representations and grants in this Release.

6.2 **Survival.** The consent in this Section survives the Model reaching the age
of majority and remains binding.

## 7. Indemnity and Limitation

7.1 **Producer indemnity for misuse.** Producer will be responsible for its own
use of the Images that exceeds the scope of this Release or violates the
limitations in Section 2.3.

7.2 **Model representations.** The Model will be responsible for any breach of the
representations in Section 4, including the right to grant the rights in this
Release.

7.3 **Limitation of liability.** Except for the matters above, neither Party is
liable to the other for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive
damages arising out of this Release.

## 8. General Provisions

8.1 **Governing law and venue.** This Release is governed by the laws of the State
of **[STATE]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws rules. The Parties submit to
the jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in **[COUNTY, STATE]**.

8.2 **Entire agreement; amendment.** This Release is the entire agreement between
the Parties on its subject and may be amended only by a writing signed by both
Parties.

8.3 **Severability and waiver.** If any provision is unenforceable, the rest
remains in effect, and the unenforceable provision will be narrowed to the extent
needed to make it enforceable. A Party's failure to enforce a provision is not a
waiver.

8.4 **Binding effect.** This Release binds and benefits the Parties and their
heirs, successors, licensees, and assigns.

8.5 **Counterparts and electronic signature.** This Release may be signed in
counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and all of
which together form one agreement.
