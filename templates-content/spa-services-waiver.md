This Spa Services Waiver and Release (this "**Waiver**") is entered into between
**[SPA LEGAL NAME]**, a **[STATE] [ENTITY TYPE]** doing business as
**[SPA TRADE NAME]**, located at **[SPA ADDRESS]** (the "**Spa**"), and the
individual signing below (the "**Client**"). By signing, the Client agrees to the
terms below before receiving spa services.

**Client information:** Name **[CLIENT NAME]**; Date of birth **[DOB]**; Phone
**[PHONE]**; Email **[EMAIL]**.

**Recitals.** The Client wishes to receive one or more spa, beauty, or wellness
services from the Spa. Such services carry inherent risks. The Spa is willing to
provide the services in exchange for the Client's acknowledgments, assumption of
risk, and release set out below. In consideration of being permitted to receive
the services, the Client agrees as follows.

---

## 1. Services and Acknowledgment of Risks

1.1 **Services.** The Client requests the following service(s) (the "**Services**"):
**[E.G. MASSAGE, FACIAL, BODY TREATMENT, WAXING, CHEMICAL PEEL, SAUNA, NAIL
SERVICE, OTHER]**.

1.2 **Inherent risks.** The Client understands that the Services may involve risks,
which depending on the Service may include skin irritation, redness, allergic
reaction, bruising, breakouts, temporary discomfort, sensitivity to heat or
products, burns from heat or wax, slips on wet surfaces, or aggravation of
existing conditions. The Client voluntarily accepts these inherent risks.

1.3 **No guarantee of results.** The Client understands that individual results
vary and that the Spa does not guarantee any particular cosmetic or therapeutic
result.

1.4 **Not medical care.** The Client understands the Services are for relaxation,
beauty, or general wellness and are not a substitute for medical diagnosis or
treatment. The Spa's personnel do not diagnose medical conditions or prescribe
treatment unless separately licensed and expressly engaged to do so.

## 2. Health Disclosure

2.1 **Accurate information.** The Client will provide accurate and complete
information about the Client's health, allergies, medications, skin sensitivities,
pregnancy, recent procedures, and any condition that could affect the safety of
the Services.

2.2 **Conditions to disclose.** The Client confirms that the Client has disclosed
any of the following that apply: **[ALLERGIES; PREGNANCY; HIGH/LOW BLOOD PRESSURE;
HEART CONDITION; DIABETES; SKIN CONDITIONS; RECENT SURGERY OR INJURY; MEDICATIONS
INCLUDING RETINOIDS OR BLOOD THINNERS; OTHER]**.

2.3 **Changes and reactions.** The Client will promptly tell the provider about any
discomfort, pain, or adverse reaction during a Service, and will follow reasonable
aftercare instructions. The Client will report any reaction after a Service to the
Spa promptly.

2.4 **Reliance.** The Client understands the Spa relies on the Client's disclosures
to provide the Services safely, and that withholding or misstating information may
increase the Client's risk.

## 3. Assumption of Risk and Release

3.1 **Assumption of risk.** Having been informed of the risks, the Client knowingly
and voluntarily assumes all risks associated with the Services, whether or not
specifically described in this Waiver, except as limited by Section 3.3.

3.2 **Release.** To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, the Client
releases and discharges the Spa and its owners, employees, and contractors (the
"**Released Parties**") from claims, demands, and liabilities arising from the
Client's receipt of the Services, except to the extent they result from the
Released Parties' gross negligence or willful misconduct.

3.3 **What is not released.** This Waiver does not release liability that cannot be
released under applicable law, including, where applicable, liability for gross
negligence, willful misconduct, or personal injury that the law does not permit to
be waived. The enforceability of liability waivers varies by jurisdiction; some
provisions may be limited or unenforceable under local law.

3.4 **Indemnification.** To the extent permitted by law, the Client will indemnify
the Released Parties against third-party claims arising from the Client's breach of
this Waiver or the Client's failure to disclose relevant health information.

## 4. Aftercare and Client Responsibilities

4.1 **Aftercare.** The Client agrees to follow the aftercare instructions provided
for the Services, including any guidance on sun exposure, products to avoid, and
activity restrictions.

4.2 **Honest feedback.** The Client will communicate openly about comfort,
pressure, temperature, and sensitivity so the provider can adjust the Service.

4.3 **Personal property.** The Client is responsible for the Client's personal
belongings. The Spa is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged personal
property except to the extent caused by the Spa's gross negligence.

## 5. Appointments, Cancellations, and Payment

5.1 **Cancellation policy.** The Client agrees to the Spa's cancellation policy:
**[E.G. 24 HOURS' NOTICE REQUIRED; LATE CANCELLATION OR NO-SHOW MAY BE CHARGED
[AMOUNT OR PERCENTAGE]]**.

5.2 **Payment.** The Client will pay the posted or quoted price for the Services,
plus applicable taxes and any agreed gratuity, at the time of service unless
otherwise arranged.

5.3 **Right to refuse service.** The Spa may decline or stop a Service if, in its
reasonable judgment, providing it would be unsafe or inappropriate, including
where the Client's disclosures indicate a contraindication.

## 6. Media and Privacy

6.1 **Photographs.** The Spa **[MAY / MAY NOT]** take before-and-after or treatment
photographs. If permitted, the Spa will use them only for the purposes the Client
authorizes below.

6.2 **Marketing consent.** The Client **[CONSENTS / DOES NOT CONSENT]** to the Spa
using non-identifying photographs of the Client's treatment results for marketing.
Identifiable use requires the Client's separate written consent.

6.3 **Privacy.** The Spa will handle the Client's personal and health information
in accordance with its privacy practices and applicable law, and will not sell the
Client's personal information.

## 7. General Provisions

7.1 **Adult or guardian.** The Client is at least the age of majority in the
governing jurisdiction. If the Client is a minor, a parent or legal guardian must
sign on the Client's behalf and assumes the obligations of this Waiver.

7.2 **Voluntary agreement.** The Client has read this Waiver, has had the
opportunity to ask questions, understands it, and signs it voluntarily.

7.3 **Governing law and venue.** This Waiver is governed by the laws of the State
of **[STATE]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws rules. Any dispute will be
resolved in the courts located in **[COUNTY, STATE]**.

7.4 **Severability.** If any provision of this Waiver is held unenforceable, the
remaining provisions continue in effect, and the unenforceable provision will be
enforced to the greatest extent permitted by law.

7.5 **Entire agreement.** This Waiver is the entire agreement between the Parties
on its subject and supersedes prior discussions. It may be amended only in a
writing signed by both.

7.6 **Electronic signature.** This Waiver may be signed by electronic signature,
which has the same effect as a handwritten signature.
