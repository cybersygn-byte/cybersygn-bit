Questions.

The ones we get most.

Honest answers. If yours is not here, write to hello@cybersygn.io and we will add it.

How accurate is field detection?

100% on our regression set of 37 real-world contracts and 10 synthetic test PDFs. The detector walks the PDF operators and matches labeled patterns including Signature:, /s/, (Initial), Date:, X______, and [ ]. Word documents are converted to PDF in the browser before detection.

Does the signer need an account?

No. Signers click a unique magic link and sign in the browser. No signup, no password, no app to install.

Is CyberSygn a DocuSign alternative?

Yes. CyberSygn replaces DocuSign, Dropbox Sign, and SignWell for the case where you do not want to drag and drop signature fields onto every document. The detection step is what is different. Every other capability (multi-signer routing, magic links, audit trail, signed PDF download) is parity with the competition.

What happens to my PDF after I upload it?

Detection runs in your browser. The PDF never leaves your device during the detection step. When you invite remote signers, the original PDF is stored in Cloudflare KV with a 30-day TTL, encrypted at rest, and auto-deleted after 30 days unless you download it.

Is the audit certificate legally binding?

It documents the audit trail (every event timestamp, IP, user agent, and a SHA-256 fingerprint of the original PDF). Legal weight depends on jurisdiction. We are not lawyers. The certificate is evidence, not a guarantee.

What does it cost?

Free for 3 documents per month. Solo is $12 per month for unlimited documents. Founding is $9 per month locked for life, first 100 customers only. Team is $29 per month for 3 seats.

Do you support Word documents?

Yes. Word files are converted to PDF in your browser before detection runs, so the entire pipeline post-upload is PDF-native.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Manage your subscription from the dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. No phone call required.
