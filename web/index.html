How it works.

Three steps. In this order.

Upload a PDF. CyberSygn finds the fields. Pick in-person signing on the spot, or send private links to remote signers. You get the signed PDF and a tamper-evident audit certificate the moment the last signer completes.

  1. 01 · Upload

    Drop a PDF or scan one in.

    Any PDF works. Or photograph paper documents with your camera and we'll compose them into a PDF locally. Detection then runs in your browser and highlights every field.

  2. 02 · Route

    Sign in person, or send.

    Pass the iPad around if your signers are present. Or route a private link to each remote signer. No accounts, no passwords, no setup on their end.

  3. 03 · Close

    Get the signed PDF and proof.

    You get the flattened PDF plus a tamper-evident audit certificate with every event, IP, and a SHA-256 fingerprint, the moment the last signer completes.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. From your dashboard, one click, no email required. You keep access through the end of the billing period. Origin members lose the $9-for-life lock if they cancel, but every other tier is fully reversible.

Is it legally binding?

Yes. CyberSygn is ESIGN Act (United States) and UETA-compliant. The audit certificate captures every signing event, IP address, timestamp, and the SHA-256 fingerprint of the original document. Same legal weight as DocuSign or HelloSign in any U.S. jurisdiction.

No, ever. Free-tier signups consent to anonymous, content-free field-position data being added to our detection-improvement corpus. Paid tiers opt out by default. Signed PDFs are stored long enough for the signing flow to complete, then expire. Read the full privacy policy.

No. Signers click a unique magic link and sign in their browser. No signup, no password, no app to install. The most-hated part of DocuSign-style flows is the part we removed.

Questions.

The ones we get most.

Honest answers. If yours is not here, write to hello@cybersygn.io and we will add it.

How accurate is field detection?

100% on our regression set of 37 real-world contracts and 10 synthetic test PDFs. The detector walks the PDF operators and matches labeled patterns including Signature:, /s/, (Initial), Date:, X______, and [ ]. Word documents are converted to PDF in the browser before detection.

Does the signer need an account?

No. Signers click a unique magic link and sign in the browser. No signup, no password, no app to install.

Is CyberSygn a DocuSign alternative?

Yes. CyberSygn replaces DocuSign, Dropbox Sign, and SignWell for the case where you do not want to drag and drop signature fields onto every document. The detection step is what is different. Every other capability (multi-signer routing, magic links, audit trail, signed PDF download) is parity with the competition.

What happens to my PDF after I upload it?

Detection runs in your browser. The PDF never leaves your device during the detection step. When you invite remote signers, the original PDF is stored in Cloudflare KV with a 30-day TTL, encrypted at rest, and auto-deleted after 30 days unless you download it.

Is the audit certificate legally binding?

It documents the audit trail (every event timestamp, IP, user agent, and a SHA-256 fingerprint of the original PDF). Legal weight depends on jurisdiction. We are not lawyers. The certificate is evidence, not a guarantee.

What does it cost?

Demo is free for 3 documents lifetime, no card required. Solo is $12 per month for unlimited documents. Origin is $9 per month locked for the life of your account, limited to the first 100 founders. Studio is $29 per month for a 3-seat team with shared workspace and member roles.

Do you support Word documents?

Yes. Word files are converted to PDF in your browser before detection runs, so the entire pipeline post-upload is PDF-native.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Manage your subscription from the dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. No phone call required.
