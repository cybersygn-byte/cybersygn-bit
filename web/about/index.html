The why.

Why a new e-signature tool?

I sign a lot of contracts. NDAs, contractor agreements, leases, photo releases. Every time, I'd watch myself spend twenty minutes dragging signature boxes onto a page that had a perfectly clear signature line already printed on it. The slowest part of signing anything was telling the tool where the fields were that I could already see with my eyes.

That broke me. So I built the thing I wanted: drop a PDF, watch the fields appear, send it, get it back signed. Detection runs locally in your browser — your document doesn't leave your machine until you choose to send it. The signer clicks a link and signs. No account, no app, no friction.

CyberSygn is built on Cloudflare Workers, edge-deployed, fast everywhere. It operates under the same ESIGN Act and UETA frameworks every reputable e-signature platform does. Same legal weight, a fraction of the time, a fraction of the price.
