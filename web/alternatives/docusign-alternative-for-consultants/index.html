The documents.

The contracts consultants actually sign.

CyberSygn detection passes on every document type a consulting practice uses, including the long-tail forms that always have a checkbox buried on page seven.

  • Statement of work. Multi-page, signature line, initials on every page, deliverable checkboxes.
  • Master service agreement. Signature, witness signature, effective date, "I have read the attached" checkbox.
  • Engagement letter. One signature, one date, the client's printed name. The shortest one we see.
  • Mutual NDA. Two signatures, two dates, term-length checkbox, optional jurisdiction line.
  • Change order. Reference number, revised scope, signature on both sides.
  • IP assignment rider. Signature, date, work-product description, optional witness.
