CyberSygn / Refund policy

Refund policy.

Thirty days. No questions.

Last updated: May 30, 2026.

The short version.

Within thirty days of your first paid month on any plan, email us and we will refund the full amount you paid us. We will not ask why. We will not try to talk you out of it. Refunds go back to the original card, typically within five business days, sometimes sooner depending on your bank.

How to request a refund.

Email billing@cybersygn.io from the address on your CyberSygn account. Include the word "refund" in the subject line. That is the whole process.

You can also cancel your subscription from the dashboard at any time. Cancellation alone does not trigger a refund; you have to email. Cancellation stops the next charge.

What we refund.

  • The first paid month, in full, within thirty days of that charge.
  • A duplicate charge, if our billing system makes one.
  • A charge after you cancelled, if our system fails to honor the cancellation.

What we do not refund.

  • Any month after the first thirty days. Cancel anytime and the next month does not bill, but past months stay billed.
  • Annual plans (we do not currently sell any; this is a placeholder for if we add them later, with the same thirty-day window).
  • Charges to your Stripe account for documents you actually sent. Stripe processing fees on refunded charges are absorbed by us, not by you.
  • Anything outside thirty days, unless required by consumer law where you live (UK, EU, California consumers have additional rights).

Founding members.

If you refund within the first thirty days of a Founding subscription, you give up the $9 locked rate. If you come back later, you join Solo at $12. We hold one hundred Founding slots and a refunded slot returns to the available pool.

Team accounts.

Same thirty-day window. Refund covers the whole Team subscription; individual seat-level refunds are not separate, since billing is at the workspace level.

If we cancel you.

If we terminate your account for a reason other than your breach of our terms, we refund the unused portion of your current billing period prorated to the day of termination. Refunds for accounts terminated for material breach (fraud, abuse, etc.) are at our discretion.

If you are in the EU, UK, or California.

You have additional statutory cancellation and refund rights under your local consumer protection laws. Nothing in this policy is meant to limit those rights. If the law where you live entitles you to a longer refund window or different terms, those apply.

Disputes.

Please email us before you charge back. Chargebacks cost us bank fees and often hurt future customers because they raise our processor's risk score. If we have not refunded you within five business days of your request, that is on us; tell us and we will fix it.

Contact.
