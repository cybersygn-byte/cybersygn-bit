# Alternatives-directory backlink campaign

Submit CyberSygn to "DocuSign alternative" directories. Each listing is
a referral pipeline + a backlink. Submission templates below. Do these
in the week leading up to the PH/HN launch — they take 5-15 minutes
each, compound for years.

Once you're listed on G2/Capterra, encourage Origin customers to leave
a review. Even 3-5 reviews dramatically boost your discoverability
within the directory. Offer Origin customers a Studio-tier upgrade
or a month of credit in exchange for an honest review.
