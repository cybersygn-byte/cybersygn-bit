## The honest take

If you sign one or two contracts a month, the free tiers work. CyberSygn's Demo gives you three lifetime (not per month — lifetime), which is enough to evaluate seriously. HelloSign and SignWell give you three per month but make your signer create an account, which is the worst part of DocuSign.

If you sign five or more contracts a month, every option except the free tiers becomes worth it. The question is which one matches your workflow.

## What the price hides

DocuSign at $25/month sounds reasonable until you realize the Personal tier caps you at 5 documents. If you send 6, you're forced up to Standard at $35/month. That's a 40% price jump for one extra document.

CyberSygn doesn't do per-document caps on paid tiers. Send as many as you want.

HelloSign at $20/month is the closest direct competitor to CyberSygn Solo. The difference: HelloSign makes you place every field manually, exactly like DocuSign. CyberSygn finds them for you. The time savings alone, at $60/hour for a typical solo, pays for the subscription difference inside the first contract.

## What's actually free forever

- **Adobe Acrobat Reader** can self-sign unlimited documents at $0. You can't send to other people for signing, but if you only need to sign things people send you, this is sufficient.
- **Preview on macOS** does the same thing as Acrobat Reader for self-signing, with a slightly nicer signature-capture UX.
- **CyberSygn Demo** gives you three free sends (to anyone, with the audit certificate) lifetime.

## What I'd actually pick

If I'm setting up my own freelance business in 2026 from scratch:

1. **First 30 days**: CyberSygn Demo (3 free sends) to validate the workflow.
2. **Months 2-3**: CyberSygn Origin at $9/mo locked for life. The Origin tier is capped at 100 founders; once it's gone, the rate jumps to $12. Even at $12 it's still half of DocuSign.
3. **If I grow into a small team**: CyberSygn Studio at $29/mo for 3 seats.

Why this stack: every paid tier in the table above except CyberSygn makes someone (you or your client) do work that software should do. The field-detection is the difference between a 20-minute send and a 3-second send.

## What I wouldn't pick

DocuSign Personal at $25/mo for 5 documents a month. That's $5/document. The math gets worse as you grow.
